FAQs

Can't find what you're looking for? Talk to our team →

OptiNode ships with a library of 50+ pre-trained defect detection models covering common defect categories across industries. For edge cases, our active learning pipeline automatically selects and labels high-value samples from your production stream — so your model improves continuously without manual annotation effort.
OptiNode is hardware-agnostic. We support GigE Vision, USB3 Vision, MIPI CSI-2, and most IP camera protocols via RTSP. Pre-validated integrations exist for Basler, IDS, Cognex, and Allied Vision cameras. The edge node runs on NVIDIA Jetson, standard x86 with GPU, or cloud VMs.
Most customers are running their first inspection pipeline within 2–4 hours of signing up, using a pre-trained model. Full production deployment with custom model tuning typically takes 1–3 weeks, depending on the number of defect classes and available hardware.
Yes. OptiNode exposes a REST API, MQTT broker, and OPC-UA server out of the box. Pre-built connectors exist for Siemens TIA Portal, Rockwell FactoryTalk, SAP MES, and most major SCADA platforms. Enterprise customers receive a dedicated integration engineer during onboarding.
By default, OptiNode processes video frames on your edge hardware and only sends metadata (defect class, confidence, timestamp, unit ID) to the cloud dashboard. Raw frames are never stored by default. Enterprise plans support fully air-gapped on-premise deployments where no data leaves your facility.
